A little about me

Hey, I’m Arielle, a User Experience Designer with a passion for creating thoughtful online experiences that balance user needs with business goals.

I have always worked in creative industries. I began as a Fashion Designer and then worked as a Graphic Designer. UX design was a logical next step for me, given that research and adaptability have always been fundamental to my design practice.

I firmly believe that innovative design is driven by collaboration and a continuous practice of learning. For this reason, I am always seeking a challenge and value the opportunity to work with a team.

My strengths and passions lie in sketching, information architecture and prototyping as ways to express my thoughts and ideas.

As a UX designer I bring together my knowledge of design with my understanding and appreciation of the user thought processes to make seamless and innovative experiences.
