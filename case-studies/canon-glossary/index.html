Canon Glossary

An online photography glossary

The Client

Canon Australia, the leading producer of Digital Cameras, SLR Cameras, Professional Printers, Scanners & Fax Machines for both business & home users.

The Team

Being the sole UX designer on this project, I worked alongside the Client, Project Manager, Developers, and Visual Designers to bring this project to life.

Skills, Techniques and Tools used

  • Information Architecture
  • Sketching
  • Wireframes (Illustrator)
  • Concept development
  • Stakeholder management

In Summary – The Brief

We were approached to create a glossary of technical photogrpahy terms that out-rank Google's current search results. Additionally, the Glossary was to position Canon strategically into becoming the answer to the photography market's questions and to "go to" online resource for all definitions.

In Summary – The Solution

A beautiful, interactive and exploratory glossary that answers questions quickly whilst also exceeding the users' expectations by offering deeper and more interesting information than other online competitors.
