Canon Colour Challenge

A responsive promotional microsite

The Client

Canon Australia, the leading producer of Digital Cameras, SLR Cameras, Professional Printers, Scanners & Fax Machines for both business & home users.

The Team

Being the sole UX designer on this project, I worked alongside the Client, Project Manager, Developers, and Visual Designers to bring this project to life.

Skills, Techniques and Tools used

  • Sketching
  • Wireframes (using Illustrator)
  • User flows for requesting a pack of cards
  • Concept development
  • Stakeholder management
  • Usability testing

In Summary – The Brief

Canon Colour Challenge welcomes new Canon camera owners into a world of colour with a deck of cards that challenges them to mix things up.

In Summary – The Solution

I constructed a microsite that allowed people to request a pack of cards, share their images and explore a gallery of photographs taken by the Canon Colour Challenge community.
