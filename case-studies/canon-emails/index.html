Canon Emails

A series of onboarding and welcome eDMs

The Client

Canon Australia, the leading producer of Digital Cameras, SLR Cameras, Professional Printers, Scanners & Fax Machines for both business & home users.

Timeline

1 week

The Team

Being the sole UX designer on this project, I worked alongside the Client, Strategist, Project Manager, Developers, Copywriters and Visual Designers to bring this project to life.

Skills, Techniques and Tools used

  • Best Practice Research
  • Information Architecture
  • Sketching
  • Wireframes (using Illustrator)
  • Concept development
  • Stakeholder management

In Summary – The Brief

To create a series of on-boarding and welcome eDMs for Canon's new ECRM program.

In Summary – The Solution

A series of 9 emails aimed at new users who have signed up to Canon through various avenues. The main purpose of these emails was to compel users to take action and evolve themselves with either their new product or the Canon community.

I did an extensive amount of research on welcoming emails, on-boarding emails and general eDM best practices; this was to ensure that I created the best possible experience with regards to content and design.

The above research helped me construct the wireframes and inform the best times to distribute the eDMs to users within a 6 month period.
