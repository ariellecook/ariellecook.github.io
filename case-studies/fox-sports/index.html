Fox Sports

A reponsive product comparison site

The Client

Fox Sports, Australia's leading sports distribution channel.

Timeline

UX: 2 weeks
Design: 2.5 weeks

The Team

Being a UX Designer and Visual Designer on this project, I worked alongside the Client, Project Manager, and Developers.

Skills, Techniques and Tools Used

  • Information Architecture
  • Sketching
  • Wireframes (Illustrator & Omnigraffle)
  • Competitive & Comparitive Analysis
  • User Research
  • Stakeholder Management
  • Design (Photoshop & Illustrator)

In Summary – The Brief

To update the existing look, feel and functionality o the current Fox Sports package selector site.

In Summary – The Solution

A full responsive website from desktop through to mobile, with a more functional and engaging experience than the previous version.

Competitive & Comparitive Analysis

I conducted a competitive analysis to discover what types of functionality suited users best when comparing products. From this, I came to the conclusion that when comparing products people want a clear, simple layout. The challenge was to then find out what would help engage users without being too prominent and distracting.

After conducting a heuristic evaluation on the current site, my solution was to utilise the user query to link the type of sport they were looking at previously into the header image and make that particular sport more prominent within the comparison chart.

I began by sketching the wireframes, which I tested and iterated on many times.

I then produced a high fidelity mobile mockup using Illustrator, HTML and CSS for a presentation with the stakeholders to show key interactions within the app.

Having supplied the client with 2 UX options with both major and minor changes to the existing site, the client purchased both concepts to roll out at different stages. After the 2 concepts were approved, I then brought the wireframes to life with a motivating, on-brand interface design.

Furthermore, this package selector went on to become the primary means of overall acquisition for Foxtel and Fox Sports.
