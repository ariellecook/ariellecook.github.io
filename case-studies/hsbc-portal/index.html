HSBC

Document upload portal

The Client

HSBC, a multinational banking and financial servies company.

Timeline

2 months

The Team

Being the sole UX designer on this project, I worked alongside the Client, Project Manager, Developers, and Visual Designers to bring this project to life.

Skills, Techniques and Tools used

  • Information Architecture
  • User Journeys
  • Sketching
  • Wireframes (Axure)
  • Stakeholder Management

In Summary – The Brief

HSBC engaged us to re-develop and design a streamlined process for uploading verification documents required across a range of HSBC online applications. The business objective was to maximise customer usage and completion within the document upload portal.

In Summary – The Solution

A stand-alone mobile responsive design named "Image & Attach". Image & Attach was designed to be self-explanatory and autonomous (i.e. not relying on other online journeys to inform and/or insruct the user on how the portal functions).

The ourput from this project also included a user journey review, strategic recommendations, and wireframes.

The Outcome

After HSBC introduced the new "Image & Attach" design within the Australian market, the success rate was so much higher than the previous version that it was rolled out globally.
