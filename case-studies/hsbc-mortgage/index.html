HSBC

Online mortgage application

In Summary – The Brief

To redesign the experience of applying for an online mortgage application with HSBC Australia, without altering any existing functionality. The objective was to maximise application conversion rates (app start to app submit)

In Summary – The Solution

We developed a user-centric Approval in Principal online application form that could easily be completed by customers. The concept transformed the arduous and one way process of filling out a form into a useful and encouraging portal space that hosted a customer's mortgage application. This project had a fast turnaround time, working in an Agile format through discovery, concepting and wireframing sprint phases.

Concept Creation

I helped construct a heuristic evaluation of the current site and researched the compettitor landscape in order to define key recommendations. Stakeholder WIP meetings were held regularly to ensure scope focus and deliver key research findings.

Site Maps

After looking at the current online journey, we were able to incorporate the recommendations that we made into a sitemap.

Wireframes

Keeping in mind the broad spectrum of users that would be engaging with this app, we started to wireframe the main user journeys screens (apply, retrieve and response) in Axure. During this process we designed the steps in line with style guidelines.

Usability testing

Using our interactive Axure prototype, we tested the design with 4 users in each sprint, taking on their feedback and iterating the designs along the way.
